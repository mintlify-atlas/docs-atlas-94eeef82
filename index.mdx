## What it does

Brandon's Matchups is a single-page Next.js app that tracks your personalized March Madness bracket. Each matchup card shows:

- **Your team** vs. their team, with seeds
- **Live score** for both sides
- **Spread** (e.g., `+8.5` or `-2.5`) color-coded by role
- **Role badge** — Underdog, Favorite, or Pending
- **Game metadata** — tip time, broadcast network, date, and region
- **Head-to-head opponent** in your bracket pool

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your bracket data">
    Drop your pool CSV into Claude to extract your relevant matchups. See the [data pipeline guide](/how-it-works/data-pipeline).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy to Vercel">
    Push the repo to GitHub and connect it to Vercel for automatic deployments on every update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure automated sync">
    Set up Codex automations to scrape scores and spreads throughout the tournament. See [sync automation](/how-it-works/sync-automation).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch it update">
    Every time Codex pushes updated matchup data, Vercel rebuilds and redeploys automatically — no manual refreshes needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This is personal, experimental software — not a multi-tenant SaaS product. It's designed to be forked, personalized, and run for one person's bracket pool.
</Note>
